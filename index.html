We partner with founders and product teams to turn high-stakes ideas into products that launch, grow, and earn user trust. Every engagement is built around clear outcomes, fast decision-making, and design that moves the business forward.

Two Designers. One Standard.

The Product Lift is led by Shashank Seth and Roopal Gupta, two senior product designers who work directly with founders and product teams from strategy through shipped interface.

Shashank brings execution depth across AI, fintech, edtech, and hospitality from Quizizz, NVIDIA, Toast, and GetMega. Roopal brings strength in UX research, systems thinking, and consumer product design across Hotstar, Salarybox, CSEWhy, and travel products.

We keep engagements intentionally focused, with direct collaboration, fast decision cycles, and production-ready outputs that improve product clarity, team velocity, and user outcomes.
