Earnnest: Designing the Future of Alternative Investing in India

When I joined Earnnest, there was no brand, no interface, and no existing user base - only a vision to make real estate-backed debt investing accessible and trustworthy for everyday Indians. I led the end-to-end product design across brand identity, website, web app, and mobile app.

Executive Snapshot

Earnnest was entering a high-trust category with a low-trust starting point: no brand equity, no UX baseline, and a complex investment product unfamiliar to most retail users.

Business Goal

Launch a credible digital investment product that can convert cautious first-time investors while still meeting HNI expectations.

Core Product Bet

Use clarity and trust cues as growth levers, not visual flourish, by reducing ambiguity at every financial decision point.

My Scope

Brand identity, website, web app, mobile app, onboarding journeys, investment flow, and dashboard system.

Timeframe

3 months, solo product design ownership across strategy, UX, UI, and handoff.

Live: earnnest.me

Research to Product Strategy

Initial discovery showed that users did not reject the product economics, they rejected uncertainty. Most drop-off was trust-related, not intent-related.

  • 10+ user interviews: Ages 28-65, focused on risk perception and trust language.
  • Competitive teardown: Altifi, Grip Invest, Strata, with conversion and copy benchmarks.
  • Compliance workshops: Legal constraints translated into UX rules before UI design started.
  • Behavioral insight: Mobile-first users wanted certainty in plain English before return optimization.

What Changed in the Experience

Information Model

Reframed deal cards around risk, lock-in, payout timeline, and collateral signals before showcasing projected returns.

Onboarding Flow

Converted KYC into a progressive, step-confirmed journey with clear completion states and contextual help.

Investment Flow

Reduced commit path to four mobile-friendly steps with disclosure clarity at the exact decision moment.

Portfolio Layer

Added payout and milestone visibility so investors could track outcomes without support dependency.

Impact Snapshot

+32% Lead conversion uplift after website and information hierarchy redesign.
8,000+ Monthly active users within 6 weeks of launch.
24% Investment conversion from deal browse to committed flow.

Key Funnel Improvements

Homepage Engagement2.4x
KYC Completion+38%
Investment Conversion24%

Critical Challenges and Resolutions

  • Asset-class unfamiliarity: Introduced progressive explainers with examples tied to user intent moments.
  • Regulatory complexity: Surfaced disclosures in context, avoiding long legal walls that hurt completion.
  • KYC drop-off: Added step indicators, autofill, and live-state validation to reduce uncertainty.
  • Mobile performance: Simplified SVG and card rendering patterns for smoother Android behavior.

Key Takeaways

  • Trust is a product behavior problem, not just a branding problem.
  • Funnel gains came from clearer decisions, not extra feature depth.
  • Early legal-product alignment prevented expensive redesign loops.
  • In fintech 0-to-1, micro-clarity outperforms visual novelty.
